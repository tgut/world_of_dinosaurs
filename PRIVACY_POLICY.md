# Privacy Policy | 隐私政策

## English Version

### Last Updated: April 2026

**World of Dinosaurs** ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our mobile application.

---

#### 1. Information We Collect

##### 1.1 Permissions Required

The App requests the following device permissions to provide its features:

- **Camera Permission**: Used for:
  - Viewing dinosaur 3D models through Augmented Reality (AR)
  - Scanning QR codes to unlock dinosaur information
  - Image-based dinosaur recognition feature

- **Microphone Permission**: Used for:
  - Voice input in the AI Chat feature
  - Voice command functionality

- **Internet Permission**: Used for:
  - API communications with external services (detailed below)
  - Data synchronization
  - Feature content delivery

##### 1.2 Data We Collect

- **Usage Data**: Dinosaur pages viewed, features used, quiz performance (stored locally on your device)
- **Settings Preferences**: Language selection, theme preference, voice settings (stored locally)
- **Chat Conversations**: Messages you send through the AI Chat feature (see Section 3 for API provider details)
- **Camera/Audio Input**: Temporary processing for AR viewing and voice recognition (processed but not stored without your consent)
- **Device Information**: App version, OS version, device type (for crash reporting and analytics, if enabled)

**Local Storage**: Most user data (dinosaur information, favorites, quiz history, preferences) is stored locally on your device.

---

#### 2. How We Use Your Information

- **Provide Core Features**: Display dinosaur information, 3D models, AR experiences, timeline, and quizzes
- **AI Chat Service**: Your messages are sent to third-party AI providers to generate responses
- **Voice Recognition**: Your audio input is processed by device-level speech recognition (locally) or cloud services
- **Image Recognition**: Photos you take are sent to Google Vision API for dinosaur identification (if this feature is enabled)
- **Maps Service**: Location data is used only within the app for the Discovery Map feature
- **App Improvement**: Anonymous usage patterns help us improve features and fix bugs
- **Error Reporting**: Crash logs are collected to improve app stability (optional, can be disabled in settings)

---

#### 3. Third-Party Services

Our App integrates with the following external services. Data you provide to these services is governed by **their respective privacy policies**:

##### 3.1 AI Chat Providers

Users can select from the following AI providers for chat functionality:

- **DeepSeek** (platform.deepseek.com)
  - Chat messages are sent to DeepSeek's servers for processing
  - DeepSeek's Privacy Policy applies: https://www.deepseek.com/privacy

- **Qwen (阿里云通义千问)** (dashscope.console.aliyun.com)
  - Chat messages are sent to Alibaba Cloud's servers
  - Aliyun Privacy Policy applies: https://www.aliyun.com/service/alibabacloud_privacy.html

- **Gemini (Google)** (aistudio.google.com)
  - Chat messages are sent to Google's servers
  - Google Privacy Policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

- **Custom API Endpoints**: If users configure custom API endpoints, we are not responsible for their data handling

##### 3.2 Vision API

- **Google Cloud Vision API** is used for dinosaur image recognition
- Images you photograph are sent to Google Cloud for analysis
- Google Privacy Policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- You can review your Vision API usage in your Google Cloud Console

##### 3.3 Maps Services

- Discovery Map may use mapping services (Google Maps, Apple Maps, or similar)
- Location data is processed according to the map provider's privacy policy

##### 3.4 Text-to-Speech (TTS)

- Speech synthesis is provided by device-level TTS engines
- Text content you request to be read aloud is processed locally or by your device's TTS provider
- No data is stored permanently

##### 3.5 Advertising (Google AdMob)

- The App displays banner advertisements and rewarded video advertisements powered by **Google AdMob**
- AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as Advertising ID), IP address, and general usage data to serve relevant ads
- You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization)
- Google AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Google Ads Data Collection: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

---

#### 4. Data Security

- **Local Encryption**: Sensitive data stored on your device can be encrypted using device-level security (depends on your Android device's encryption settings)
- **Network Security**: All API communications use HTTPS encryption
- **API Keys**: Your API keys (Vision API, Chat API) are stored in the app's secure preferences and **never shared** with us or other services

---

#### 5. Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Stored on your device until you delete the app or clear app data
- **API Call Logs**: Third-party API providers maintain logs according to **their** privacy policies; we do not retain this data on our servers
- **Crash Reports**: Retained for up to 90 days for debugging purposes (can be disabled in settings)

---

#### 6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

**This App is designed for educational purposes and may be used by children under 13.**

- We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information (PII) from children under 13
- Parental consent is required before enabling certain features:
  - Voice input and chat features display a parental consent notice
  - Image recognition features display a data collection notice
- **Parental Controls**: Parents/guardians can:
  - Review what data is being collected
  - Manage app permissions through device settings
  - Request deletion of collected data
- We comply with the **Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)**

---

#### 7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request what personal data we have collected
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data (we will delete local app data; third-party API providers handle their own data per their policies)
- **Opt-Out**: Disable specific permissions through your device settings
- **Control**: Configure which features collect data through app settings

**To exercise these rights, contact us** (see Section 10)

---

#### 8. Data Sharing

We **do NOT**:
- Sell or trade your personal data
- Share API keys or authentication tokens

**Advertising**: Google AdMob may use device identifiers to serve ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (see Section 3.5).

We **MAY**:
- Share aggregated, anonymized usage statistics (e.g., "50,000 users viewed the T-Rex page")
- Comply with law enforcement requests (with proper legal process)
- Disclose data to third-party API providers **only as necessary** to provide the services you request

---

#### 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:
- Displaying a notice in the app upon launch
- Updating the "Last Updated" date

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

---

- **In-App**: Settings → About → Contact Us

  - 与外部服务的 API 通信（详见第3节）

  - 功能内容传输

##### 1.2 我们收集的数据

- **使用数据**: 浏览过的恐龙页面、使用的功能、测验成绩（存储在您的设备本地）

- **地图服务**: 位置数据仅在应用内用于发现地图功能
- **应用改进**: 匿名使用模式帮助我们改进功能和修复错误
- **错误报告**: 收集崩溃日志以提高应用稳定性（可选，可在设置中禁用）
